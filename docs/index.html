California AIDS Drug Assistance Program

What is ADAP?

The California AIDS Drug Assistance Program (ADAP) was established in 1987 to help ensure that HIV-positive uninsured and under-insured individuals have access to their life-saving medications. Today, ADAP consists of a medication program and health insurance assistance programs. Currently, over 199 drugs are available through ADAP, and there are over 6000 pharmacies statewide where clients can access these drugs.
