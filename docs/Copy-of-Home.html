California AIDS Drug Assistance Program

CA  ADAP

The California AIDS Drug Assistance Program (ADAP) was established in 1987 to help ensure that HIV-positive uninsured and under-insured individuals have access to their life-saving medications. Today, ADAP consists of a medication program and health insurance assistance programs. Currently, over 199 drugs are available through ADAP, and there are over 6000 pharmacies statewide where clients can access these drugs.

Who's ADAP for

The AIDS Drug Assistance Programs ensure that people living with HIV/AIDS who are uninsured and under-insured have access to medication.

Are you eligible

Be a resident of California;
Have a positive HIV/AIDS diagnosis;
Be at least 18 years old;
Have an annual Modified Adjusted Gross Income (MAGI) that does not exceed 500% Federal Poverty  Level based on household size and income;
Not be fully covered by Medi-Cal or any other third party payers.
